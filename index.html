FAQ
Is there a free version of Santino?

Yes! Santino offers a completely free plan with no credit card required. You get access to core features including unlimited corporate cards, travel and expense management, and basic accounting automation — perfect for smaller teams getting started.

Are there any limits on how many cards I can issue?

On the free plan, you can issue unlimited virtual and physical cards with full issuing controls. There are no hard limits on card count across any of our plans — Pro and Enterprise users get additional controls like auto-lock and compliance enforcement.

How can we manage our Santino plan?

You can manage your Santino plan directly from your account dashboard. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Plan changes take effect immediately, and our support team is available 24/7 to assist with any billing or plan-related questions.

Can I pay for Santino Pro with Santino card?

Yes, you can use your Santino corporate card to pay for your Pro or Enterprise subscription. Simply add it as your billing method in the account settings. This makes expense tracking seamless since the subscription cost is automatically logged.
